Aftercare

In most cases, your fresh tattoo will be wrapped with bandaging. It is best to leave it on until instructed which could range anywhere from a few hours to overnight. Wash it with water and mild soap (we recommend Dove Sensitive) to loosen dried serum and rinse well. This may be needed once to twice daily for about a week.

Apply Aquaphor ointment if needed or scentless lotion once the tattoo is in a drier phase, which is usually around day 5 or 6. Keep it covered with clothing and do not expose it to sun, swimming, gym, animals, yard work, etc. Do not submerge it under water. Your tattoo can get very irritated if you are doing too much.

If it becomes red and possibly infected, call us immediately so we can make sure to get you cared for.
