Aftercare

Your fresh tattoo will be wrapped with bandaging. It is best to leave it on until instructed which could range anywhere from a few hours to overnight. Wash it with water and mild soap (we recommend Dove Sensitive) and rinse well. Do this once to twice daily for about a week. Apply Aquafor ointment after every cleaning for about a week.

After a week or so, and once the tattoo is in a drier phase, switch the Aquafor out for a scentless or sensitive lotion. Keep it covered with clothing and do not expose it to sun, swimming, gym, animals, yard work, etc. Do not submerge it under water. Your tattoo can get very irritated if you are doing too much.

If it becomes overly red, painful, or possibly infected, visit a doctor or dermatologist and feel free to call/consult us for more information.
